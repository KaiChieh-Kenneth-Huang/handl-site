What problem does Handl solve?

The process of exchanging contact information (e.g. phone number or social media) during or after a conversation may involve multiple steps, which can be tedious. Handl simplifies and streamlines this exchange process.

What does Handl do for me?

Handl delivers your contact information (phone number, name, and/or social media info) via QR codes to your new friends or acquaintances. All they have to do is to take out their phone and scan your code; no more typing and searching!

What else can Handl do?

The app also acts as a hub for your favorite social media apps, giving you quick access to various apps on the phone to help make contact exchange a breeze.
